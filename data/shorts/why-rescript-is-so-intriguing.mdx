## Aggregating my activity from around the web

As a small side project, I built a series of widgets that automatically update when I use a few of my favorite apps. At the time of writing, I have written small integrations for a movie tracking app I use and also my most recent activity on GitHub. This project had a few goals:

- Integrate third party APIs using simple abstractions
- Limit API calls to once per day
- Horizontally scrollable/responsive widgets pinned to bottom of front page
